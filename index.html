About Me

Distinguished Academic Leader in Statistical Sciences

Academic Excellence

With over 29 years of dedicated research experience and 23 years of teaching at the postgraduate level, I have established myself as a leading figure in the field of statistical sciences, particularly in reliability analysis and lifetime distributions.

Currently serving as Professor and Head of the Department of Statistics at Babasaheb Bhimrao Ambedkar University, Lucknow, I am committed to advancing statistical research and nurturing the next generation of statisticians.

My research has been widely recognized through publications in prestigious international journals, and I actively contribute to the academic community through various leadership positions and memberships in professional bodies.
